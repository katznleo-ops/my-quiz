Bellow is both the personality quiz for Shadow and Bone and an explaination of my thinking process when creating the personalities for the characters and the quiz. I also included quotes from the book to support my quiz question options.

Quote #1: Alina Starkov inspired trait Question 1

This quote takes place when Alina and Mal are on a sandskiff in the shadow fold, being attacked by the vicious monsters that live there, the volcra. The text states, "I looked up and saw...two volcra swooping down on us. I huddled over Mal, shielding his body with mine. I knew it was futile, but it was all I could offer."(Bardugo, 33). This quote shows that when the volcra attacked her best friend, Mal, Alina was instantly motivated to protect him, even at the cost of her own body, even knowing there was no hope. This supports the answer option because the quote clearly shows Alina stepping into danger to save her friend, who was definitely in major trouble.

Quote #2: Alina Starkov inspired trait Question 2

This quote takes place when Alina Starkov is in Bagra's Hut, where she is participating in one of their weekly lessons together. The text states: "I reached out to the thing within me that I'd kept hidden for so long. I'm sorry, I whispered to it. I'm sorry I left you so long in the dark. I'm sorry, but I'm ready now. I called and the light answered."(Bardugo, 182). This quote shows how Alina finally summoned her power after being unable to use it for a long time because she was harboring a deep fear from her childhood that she struggled to overcome. This supports the answer option because when Alina arrived in the palace, she experienced a life-altering situation and was reluctant to embrace the change. However, in the hut, she fights back her fear and finally unlockes her true power, showing genuine perserverance and a deep inner strength.

Quote #3: The Darkling inspired trait Question 4

This quote takes place when Alina and the Darkling are alone in a room together, where the Darkling is intimite with her and tells her that "The problem with wanting is it makes us weak."(Bardugo, 240). This quote shows the Darkling's complete obsession with control and power, as he seduced Alina through affection, but he also admits that being with and liking her makes him weak. This shows his true focus in life is power and legacy, rather then being together and happy with someone. This supports the answer choice I wrote because it shows that the Darkling does not value simple pleasures, desires, and love. Instead, he is utterly focused on what can give him more power and control, striving to build himself a lasting legacy.

The quotes and explainations above demonstrate my thinking process when it came to creating the quiz question options. The personality quiz itself is located below.
